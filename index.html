about us

Think global, Act local.

NayePankh Foundation is a UP Government registered NGO, certified under 80G and 12A. We work towards bringing positive change in the lives of underprivileged children and communities through education, skill development and emotional support. Our belief is simple — every small contribution, whether it is money, skill or time, can bring a smile to someone's life.

Our Programs

Education Support

Helping underprivileged children access quality education and learning resources.

Skill Development

Teaching practical skills to help youth build a better future for themselves.

Community Support

Supporting families in need with food, resources and emotional support.
